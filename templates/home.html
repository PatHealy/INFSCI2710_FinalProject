Common Operations:

As a busy help-desk operator, we assume you will want to get to these operations the most quickly:

  • Search through cases by status and timeframe. Use the search bar that will appear at the top of the screen.
  • Search for a customer and view products the customer has purchased. Use the search bar at the top of the screen and then click the appropriate button at the right side of the row to view those products.
  • Search for common resolutions by product. Search for a product and click the appropriate button on the right side of the row.
  • Create a new case
  • Update an existing case (including closing the case). Search for the case and click 'edit'.
  • Add a new sale or add a new customer.
  • Update a customer. Search for the customer and then click the 'edit' button on the right of the row.

Browsing!

Most browse operations can be handled on the browse page.

The browse page allows you to...

  • Casually browse any table of the database

  • Search by any column, including multiple layered searches of the same table!

  • Click the headers to re-order the contents by that column.

  • Perform more complicated searches with the light-blue buttons on each row (they say what they do!)

  • Edit or delete any given row.
